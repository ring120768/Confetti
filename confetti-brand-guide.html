Clear space & size

Always leave breathing room around the logo — at least the height of the heart's lower point (about 10% of the logo's width) clear on every side. Don't crowd it with text or graphics.

Minimum size: use the full lockup no smaller than 120px wide. Below that, switch to the app icon.

Backgrounds

The logo lives happiest on ivory or cream. On a coloured surface, use the transparent PNG. On photos or busy backgrounds, place it inside a soft cream card so it stays legible. Use the single-colour version for stamps, watermarks or where colour printing isn't available.

Do & don't

Do

  • Keep generous clear space around it
  • Use on ivory, cream or soft pastel backgrounds
  • Use the transparent PNG on colour
  • Scale it proportionally

Don't

  • Stretch, squash or rotate it
  • Recolour the wordmark or the hearts
  • Add drop shadows, outlines or effects
  • Place it on busy photos without a cream card

04 — Typography

Type that echoes the logo

An elegant serif for headlines (just like the wordmark), a friendly, highly legible sans for everything else, and a script for romantic flourishes — used sparingly. All three are free on Google Fonts.

Display / HeadlinesCormorant Garamond · 500–700
Every detail, beautifully planned
A B C D E a b c d e   1 2 3 4 5   & ?

Use for page titles, section headings and big moments. Closest free match to the Confetti wordmark. Alternative: Playfair Display for bolder headlines.

Body / InterfaceLato · 300/400/700
Clear, warm and easy to read at any size.

Lato handles everything functional — paragraphs, buttons, form fields, labels and notifications. It feels human and approachable, which keeps the app from ever feeling cold or technical.

Accent / FlourishDancing Script · 600/700
Where every detail sparkles

For the tagline and rare romantic touches (a couple's names, a celebratory moment). Never for body text or anything functional.

05 — Voice & Messaging

How Confetti speaks

Like a trusted friend who happens to be a brilliant wedding planner: warm, calm and encouraging — never pushy, never panicked, never stuffy.

We are
  • Warm, not gushing
  • Organised, not clinical
  • Celebratory, not chaotic
  • Expert, not bossy
  • Reassuring, not patronising
We avoid
  • Jargon and tech-speak
  • Pushy, salesy language
  • Creating panic about deadlines
  • Stiff, formal corporate tone
  • A wall of exclamation marks

The tagline

Where Every Detail Sparkles

Our signature line. Use it on the splash screen, marketing and the app store listing.

Elevator pitch

Planning a wedding is one of the most exciting — and overwhelming — things you'll ever do. Confetti is like having your own wedding planner on your phone: a guided checklist, smart reminders and a clear timeline that keep everything on track, so every detail sparkles and nothing slips through the cracks.
